About Us

Thrace Spare Parts has established itself as one of the leading companies in the supply and export of high-quality OEM products in the automotive sector, with over 15 years of experience and rigorous work principles. Our company offers a wide range of products—including filters, brake pads, flywheels, brake discs, and other automotive solutions—ensuring superior quality standards in both local and international markets.

Our accumulated expertise, combined with our team of specialists who closely monitor industry developments, supports our objective of delivering the most suitable solutions to meet customer expectations and guaranteeing quality assurance at every stage of the supply chain. Reliability, transparency, and integrity are among our core values, reflecting our commitment to establishing long-term business relationships.

Aligned with our corporate vision, we continuously strive for improvement and innovation to offer swift and effective solutions to our customers' evolving demands. Our quality control processes adhere to international standards, and our robust supplier network enables us to be recognized as a reliable business partner in the industry.
